Supernatural

"Monsters are real, ghosts are real too. They live inside us, and sometimes they win." - Stephen King

Supernatural films are a film genre that encompasses the themes related to gods, goddess, ghosts, apparitions, spirits, miracles, and other extraordinary phenomena.

The sub-genre supernatural planted its roots in early literature, with Gothic genre of late 18th century playing a pivotal role.

Some characteristics that are involved in the supernatural horror genre are:

  • Unseen Evil
  • Possession
  • Contact with supernatural forces
  • Otherwordly Forces
  • Speculative Fiction
